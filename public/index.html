How it works
Three steps to mindful shopping
1

Detect

As you browse, ThinkFirst silently scans the page for urgency, scarcity, countdown timers, social proof abuse, and discount pressure — all offline, in under 200ms.

2

Intervene

When manipulation risk is high, a panel appears showing what was detected, your risk score, and a reflection prompt crafted by Claude AI to help you pause.

3

Reflect

Save items to your 24-hour cooling-off list. After the timer expires you get a reminder — with a clear head, decide if you still want it.
